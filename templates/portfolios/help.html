Help • Frequently Asked Questions

What can I customize?

You can customize many parts of your portfolio. Colors, fonts, page dimensions, and many other aspects are customizable. Explore your options here.

Experiment to build the portfolio you want!

I ran out of uploads. What are my options?

  • It's very easy to obtain additional uploads by upgrading your account.
  • If you don't want to upgrade your account, you can always delete uploads to make room for others.
  • If you're already using our professional account and need even more uploads, please contact us.

How do I upload an image or video?

Simply click on the gallery you'd like to upload an image or video into. Then click on the "Upload Image" or "Upload Video" button.

How do I change the order of my images, videos, or galleries?

You can drag and drop galleries, images, or videos to rearrange them. Your changes will be stored automatically.

What is Google Analytics?

Google Analytics allows you to track how many times your profile has been viewed, among other statistics. It can be a very useful tool if you're curious or concerned about how many people have seen your work.

How do I enable Google Analytics?

  1. Create an account with Google Analytics.
  2. Add your portfolio's URL to your Google Analytics account's web property setting.
  3. Find your user account number from the Admin tab in your Google Analytics account. It should look something like UA-12345-1.
  4. Go to your portfolio's customize page and enter your Google Analytics account number.

Your portfolio should now have Google Analytics enabled. You can track your portfolio's statistics using your Google Analytics account.

How do I edit my About page?

Go to your about page and click on any of the headings to edit its content. For example, if you want to edit the "Location" part of your profile, simply click on "Location" -- this will open an inline editor. Enter your location and click save. You can edit the other fields in a similar manner.

Also note that fields which display Currently Empty will not be shown publicly.

You can also upload, change, or delete your profile picture.

How do I enable or disable my Blog or Contact page?

  • Enable your Blog by entering its URL in the customize form. Disable it by clearing its URL in the form.
  • Your Contact form is enabled by default. You can change its settings within the customize form as well.
